Space Weather and Its Impact on Humanity

In this project, we review some studies on space weather to explain the physical processes that cause space weather and the effects of solar activity and its interaction with Earth, and to improve forecasting in order to improve outcomes and minimize or avoid losses as much as possible.

About Space Weather

Space weather is a condition influenced by the Sun, causing changes in the space environment around Earth. It can affect our planet in several ways:

  • It may disrupt radio and GPS signals.
  • It can impact power grids and increase the likelihood of blackouts.
  • It adds extra drag on satellites in low Earth orbit, potentially altering their paths.
  • It affects communications in aircraft and spacecraft.
  • It poses radiation hazards to astronauts.

Earth is partially protected from these effects by its magnetic field and atmosphere, but strong solar storms can still cause major disturbances to the modern technologies we rely on daily.
